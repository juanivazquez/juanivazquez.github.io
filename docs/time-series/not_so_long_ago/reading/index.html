Reading

It is a challenging task to select an appropriate number of papers on such a sophisticated subject since many ideas on time series are scattered across various authors from different backgrounds and most papers can only be understood after a systematic review. However, after some hesitation I’ve reached the conclusion that the best of my efforts should be in service to become a dedicated and good-will assistant at your service instead of trying to be a judge of relevance. In this spirit I’ve shortlisted the following texts. I hope you find value in them.
